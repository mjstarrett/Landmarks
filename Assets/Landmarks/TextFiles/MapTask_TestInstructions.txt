MAP TEST

The following map is identical to the one shown in the study phase, 
except that the targets have been removed. Use the pointer
to click and drag each store to the correct location and orientation.

There is no time limit.

When you are confident that you have correctly placed each target, 
click the button to get your score. If you do not meet the score 
criterion for this experiment, you will have another opportunity to 
study the map and retake the test.
